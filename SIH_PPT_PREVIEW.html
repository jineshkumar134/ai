The Challenge

Incomplete Ground Awareness
Early warning systems rely on remote sensors, but real-time validation from the ground (coastal residents) is missing.
Untapped Social Data
Public discourse on social media contains immediate situational insights that aren't integrated into official dashboards.
Information Chaos
High volume of misinformation spreads during coastal hazards, making verified communication difficult.

Proposed Solution

WaveWatch Platform

A deep-tech integrated ecosystem that merges official sensor data with crowdsourced ground reports and social media intelligence.

Crowdsourced Reporting
Mobile/Web portal for geotagged photo/video uploads from the impact zone.
Social Analytics Dashboard
Real-time NLP processing of Twitter/FB posts to track sentiment and identify hotspots.
